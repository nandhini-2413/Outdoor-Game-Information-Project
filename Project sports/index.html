Kabaddi

Kabaddi is a fast-paced contact sport where players raid the opponent’s territory to tag defenders and return safely — all in a single breath. It combines strategy, stamina, and teamwork, and is a popular traditional sport in South Asia.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a popular team sport where two teams of six players are separated by a net and compete to ground the ball on the opponent’s court. The game is fast-paced and demands quick reflexes, teamwork, and coordination. It is played both indoors and on beaches around the world.

Kho Kho

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams where the goal is to score the most runs. It involves batting, bowling, and fielding, and is one of the most popular sports globally, especially in countries like India, Australia, and England.
